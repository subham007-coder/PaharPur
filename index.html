We at Paharpur believe in refining people's lives and nurturing their dreams for a resilient tomorrow. The primary goal of our business is way beyond counting profits by serving our customers and other communities at large. Our corporate social responsibility stems from the ‘three bottom line’ concept entailing ‘People, Planet and Profit’ at the core of our every activity. Paharpur's commitment to measuring social, and environmental impact along with sustainability and profitable business practices are the pillars of our foundation for a better tomorrow.

We shoulder all four categories of Corporate Social Responsibility: Environmental, Ethical, Economic and Philanthropic. This page is dedicated to our philanthropic approaches towards society.

Touching Lives at
Swami Janaki Das Vidyapith

Location – West Bengal

Paharpur igniting Hopes and Education for India.
Our affiliation with Swami Janaki Das Vidyapith is a testament to our commitment to touching underprivileged lives with education and hope. Situated in the remote and distant suburbs of West Bengal, we ventured into an English Medium School with a 21st-century educational curriculum to nurture younger minds.

Touching Lives at

Location – West Bengal

Empowering Dreams with Endless Opportunities.
Our initiative equipped the school library with 1000 books, a structured staircase of the building, embedding smart class equipment, and developing a volleyball court. Making quality education accessible to marginalized communities, we transformed the future of children, igniting hopes for India.
