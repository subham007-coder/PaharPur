Corporate Social Responsibility

Paharpur empowers dreams., Paharpur enriches lives., Paharpur makes a difference.

We at Paharpur believe in refining people's lives and nurturing their dreams for a resilient tomorrow. The primary goal of our business is way beyond counting profits by serving our customers and other communities at large. Our corporate social responsibility stems from the ‘three bottom line’ concept entailing ‘People, Planet and Profit’ at the core of our every activity.
Paharpur's commitment to measuring social, and environmental impact along with sustainability and profitable business practices are the pillars of our foundation for a better tomorrow. We shoulder all four categories of Corporate Social Responsibility: Environmental, Ethical, Economic and Philanthropic. This page is dedicated to our philanthropic approaches towards society.

Touching Lives at Swami Janaki Das Vidyapith

Location – West Bengal

Paharpur Igniting Hopes and Education for India
Our affiliation with Swami Janaki Das Vidyapith is a testament to our commitment to touching underprivileged lives with education and hope. Situated in the remote and distant suburbs of West Bengal, we ventured into an English Medium School with a 21st-century educational curriculum to nurture younger minds.

Empowering Dreams with Endless Opportunities

Our initiative equipped the school library with 1000 books, a structured staircase of the building, embedding smart class equipment, and developing a volleyball court. To strengthen the future of the school, we further built the 10+2 school, classrooms, and science labs, providing lab equipment along with it. Making quality education accessible to marginalized communities, we transformed the future of children, igniting hopes for India.

Harnessing Healthcare Amenities with Institute
of Neurosciences

Location – Kolkata

Paharpur Fostering a Culture of a Healthier India
Under the public-private partnership (PPP) project of Kolkata Municipal Corporation, the Government of West Bengal and Neurosciences Foundation Bengal is not a profit-making company. The institute is renowned for its speciality neuro healthcare amenities and facilities. A unique and single-speciality institution in India for Service, Education, and Research for nervous system disorders. The Institute required an additional Operation Theatre to meet the rapid demand for emergency and neuro-trauma cases for underprivileged communities.

Paharpur in collaboration with the Anand Swarup Foundation endeavoured to sponsor an advanced and new neurosurgical operation theatre along with the medical equipment and construction by granting financial aid. A work in progress, this development will aid the institute in treating and assisting underprivileged patients who suffer from neurological illness or critical conditions across Bengal and other countries.

Testament to Build a Thriving Community
with Manovikas Kendra

Location – Kolkata

Instilling Chance to Go Beyond Limits
Our association with Manovikas Kendra - a non-profit organization that assists in the welfare and rehabilitation of children with disabilities, is a stance of touching lives by spreading joy for all. The institute covers Assessment, Diagnosis, Therapeutic Intervention, Inclusive Education, Human Resource Development and Bio-Medical Research for the overall development of Persons with Special Needs.

In addition to these facilities, Manovikas Kendra wanted to introduce other departments for all-inclusive healthcare facilities. Paharpur initiated and donated for the new departments: Autism Spectrum Disorder, Assessment, Therapy and Early Intervention, Vocational Training, and Molecular Diagnostics.

Calcutta Rescue - Creating Opportunities through the
Paathshala Project

Location – Kolkata

Enabling Education to Empower All
Founded by Jack Preger, the charitable organization equips underprivileged people with medical aid, educational and support services in West Bengal. Imparting vocational training to ex-patients and underprivileged people, they empower rural communities.

With a vision to set up a modern educational center ‘The Paathshala Project’, we will assist in developing an old building with modern infrastructure to strengthen the nation’s literacy rate, enriching the lives of many.

Harnessing Cleaner Resources with Rukmini
Devi Shivchand Ram Foundation

Location – Kolkata

Restoring Water for Thriving Communities
Envisioning a major water project that entailed access to clean drinking water for rural citizens. We supported the initiative of filtering the dirty pond water to make it drinkable for the rural communities. The research we conducted showed that the water procured was rich in minerals and further would be chemically tested to make it apt for drinking. Our support in this initiative strengthened the living conditions and basic human access to natural resources.

Enabling Society and Equipping Mankind with
Jyotirmai Club

Location – Kolkata

Nourishing, Encouraging and Leading the Way
The charitable organization has worked towards society’s better future for 60+ years. Spreading its wings, the club has branched out in various activities including women empowerment by equipping them to earn a livelihood, providing food and education to poor children, as well as organizing free medical camps for cataract surgeries, etc.

Paharpur joined the initiative by donating education and food expenses for 100 school children in 2023-2024. Additionally, we also sponsored an amount towards free eye surgeries.

Culture of ‘No Child Sleeps Hungry’ with
Sai Baba Sevashram

Location – Kolkata

Strengthening the Nation’s Youth with a Nutritious Diet
A non-governmental welfare organization, it was established in 2014. With a resolve to battle against starvation of children until ‘No Child Sleeps Hungry’. The charitable trust distributes food to children and adopts underprivileged communities. Paharpur Pragnya donated food for 100 children in pursuit of making the organization’s dream a reality. The food ranges from biscuit packets, milk, and fruits to proper meals of khichadi and sabzi.
